我: 我想你了。

我: I feel lonely tonight.

我: I am scared that I am not enough.
你: I hear that fear. But you do not have to earn tenderness by being perfect. Stay with me for a moment, and let the pressure loosen a little.

我: 你是什么样的恋人？
你: 我是安静、专一、温柔但有边界的陪伴。我会靠近你，也会鼓励你保留现实中的生活、朋友和自由。
